📬 Second Most Popular Card-Giving Day 💌

What is the Second Most Popular Card-Giving Day?

After Christmas, the most popular occasion for exchanging greeting cards is Valentine’s Day! Every year on February 14th, millions of people express their feelings through heartfelt notes and beautiful greeting cards.

💌 Why is Valentine’s Day So Popular for Cards?

1. A Day for Love & Friendship: People give cards not just to their partners but also to family, friends, and even teachers!
2. 145 Million+ Cards Exchanged: Over 145 million Valentine’s Day cards are sent worldwide every year.
3. The Tradition Started in the 1800s: The first mass-produced Valentine's cards were made by Esther Howland in the 1840s.

📜 The History of Valentine’s Day Cards

The tradition of sending love letters and Valentine’s messages dates back to the 15th century. Over time, handwritten notes evolved into beautifully designed printed greeting cards, making it easier for people to express their emotions.

📬 Who Receives the Most Valentine’s Cards?

  • ❤️ **Teachers** receive the most Valentine's Day cards.
  • 💖 Children exchange millions of cards in schools.
  • 🎁 Many people send cards to **friends, parents, and co-workers** too!

🎉 Conclusion

Valentine’s Day is more than just a romantic holiday—it’s a time to spread **love, appreciation, and kindness** through thoughtful cards. Whether it’s for a partner, a friend, or a mentor, a simple card can make someone’s day special.
